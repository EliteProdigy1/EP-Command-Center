Opening Script

"Hey, this is Jonathan with Elite Prodigy Media here on the Gulf Coast. Quick question — who handles the website for [BUSINESS]?"

If it's them:

"Perfect. The reason I'm calling — we build clean, professional websites for [INDUSTRY] businesses around Baldwin County. Five pages, mobile-ready, contact form, Google Maps, done in about 48 to 72 hours, for a flat $1,000. No contracts, no surprises. Would it be worth 10 minutes for me to show you what that would look like for [BUSINESS]?"

If they have a demo site built:

"I actually went ahead and built a full working version of your website — it's live right now and I can text you the link while we're talking. Take a look and tell me what you think."

Objections

"We already have a website."
"Totally fair — when's the last time it brought you a customer? I'll do a free side-by-side: your current site next to what we'd build. If yours wins, no hard feelings."

"That's too much money."
"One new customer likely covers it. And there's no monthly requirement — $1,000 once, you own it."

"Not right now."
"No problem. When should I check back — a month? I'll put it on my calendar." (Log as Callback.)

"Send me something."
"Even better — I'll text you a live link, not a brochure. What's the best number?"

Close + Next Step

"To get started it's a 50% deposit — $500 — Venmo or Cash App, and I'll have your draft live in 2–3 days. One round of revisions included. Sound good?"

Payment: Venmo @eliteprodigy · Cash App $ELITEPRODIGYLLC
